Ashbee Energy

A bold custom typeface system for Snickers, built from the iconic wordmark and inspired by the unapologetic attitude of American Gothic.

Challenge

Ashbee Systems has long been respected in industrial weighing technology, but as they entered the BESS and lithium-ion battery sector, they needed a fresh identity for this new vertical. The challenge was to create a modern, future-facing brand that signals energy innovation, while still retaining the legacy strength and recognition of the Ashbee name.
